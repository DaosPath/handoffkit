The Ballot and the Bridge — public localization sample
=====================================================

STORY (PG political intrigue)
  Republic of Marenth nears election day. Reporter Mira Solan
  investigates a bridge project, missing ballots, and power.

HOW TO PLAY (no RPG Maker.exe required)
  1) Open www/index.html in a browser (Chrome/Edge/Firefox).
  2) Click through dialogue. Change Lang after translate+apply.

HOW TO DEMO TRANSLATION
  hk-localize set-game <this-folder>
  hk-localize extract
  hk-localize translate --langs es,zh  # needs API key
  hk-localize apply
  Open www/index.html → choose Español / 中文 / …

Content policy: public PG sample.
